About Squid


Street QUality IDentification is a go-to built keeping in mind the issues 53,720 vehicles face who hit the streets every single day, as indicated by the statistics in an article by The Times Of India.SQUID enables a comprehensive map of a city’s street surface quality and portrays a bird’s eye view of the streets, enabling the admin to monitor the conditions in real time(pothole complications) and take informed decisions for the development and improvement of the street quality
